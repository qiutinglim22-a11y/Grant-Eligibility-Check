Company Funding Decision Tree

Follow this mapping logic to determine if the company gets up to $15k SSG funding or Absentee Payroll (AP):

Step 1: Employer Sector Check
Is the employer a Non-Government / GLC company?

• NO (Ministry / Stat Board): The company gets NO FUNDING.
• YES: Proceed to eligibility checks.

Step 2: Training & Feeder Checks
Is it a Feeder Course AND is Mode of Training = EDU/TRN?

• NO to either: Company only gets Absentee Payroll (TPG).
• YES to both: Proceed to Timeline Check.

Step 3: Timeline Check
Is the duration from Graduation/ORD to Course Start ≤ 60 months?

• NO (≥ 60 months): Company only gets Absentee Payroll (TPG).
• YES (≤ 60 months): Company gets Up to $15k SSG Grant (FMS) + AP.

Max Funding Criteria Met
Company gets SSG Grant (FMS system) up to $15,000 + Absentee Payroll.

Sign-On Incentive (SOI) Decision Tree

A trainee is eligible for the $5,000 Sign-On Incentive (SOI) if and only if ALL 5 criteria are satisfied:

Criterion 1: Feeder Course Check
Course must be a Feeder Course (YES)
Criterion 2: Mode of Training
Mode of training must be EDU / TRN
Criterion 3: Timeline Constraint
Duration from Graduation/ORD to Course Start ≤ 36 months
Criterion 4: Citizenship
Must be a Singapore Citizen (SC)
Criterion 5: BCA Scholar Status
Must NOT be a BCA Scholar (NO)
SOI Eligible
Trainee receives a one-time $5,000 Sign-On Incentive.
